Lambug Beach

Pink Beach

Zamboanga

Participating in a Zamboanga Pink Beach adventure is a must, not only for its recognition as one of the top beaches by National Geographic but also for the opportunity to explore the island's mangrove forest, ride vibrant vintas, and encounter harmless jellyfish. The beach's distinctive pink hue, a result of white sand mingling with crushed red organ pipe corals, adds to its unique charm and allure. Learn More
